As a **Cloud Infrastructure Engineer** in the project **Smart Healthcare Appointment System**, I participated in exploring AWS services, designing Cloud architecture, deploying infrastructure, configuring networking and storage components, as well as testing and reviewing the overall Cloud environment. Through these activities, I enhanced my knowledge of AWS services, Cloud architecture, infrastructure deployment, technical documentation writing, problem-solving, communication, and teamwork.

Throughout the internship, I always strove to complete assigned tasks with a strong sense of responsibility, complied with internal regulations, proactively welcomed feedback, and cooperated with team members to improve project quality.

### Areas for Improvement

* Enhance discipline and maintain better consistency in complying with schedules, internal regulations, and working procedures.
* Improve systematic problem-solving skills, particularly in analyzing technical incidents and selecting appropriate solutions.
* Elevate communication skills in daily exchanges, technical discussions, and work presentations.
* Continue practicing the ability to present technical decisions clearly and concisely.

### Lessons Learned

During the internship, I accumulated three important groups of knowledge and experience:

* **Networking:** Understood the roles of VPC, subnet, route table, Internet Gateway, NAT, Security Group, and VPC endpoint in Cloud infrastructure.
* **Practical AWS Deployment Services:** Learned how to combine AWS services to design, deploy, test, and review a real-world Cloud system.
* **Cloud Era:** Gained a broader perspective on the rapid development of Cloud computing and the importance of continuously updating technical knowledge.

I also encountered some difficulties in managing AWS costs and limiting unnecessary credit usage. Additionally, finding appropriate documentation sometimes faced obstacles due to language barriers and outdated materials. To resolve this, I sought out real-world use cases shared by the community, utilized AI tools to support documentation research, followed lab videos on YouTube, and participated in community events organized by FCAJ.

The internship helped me become more proactive in researching emerging Cloud trends and considering how to apply them to future projects.

### Development Orientation

My career objective is to become a **DevOps Engineer**. To achieve this goal, I will continue consolidating my knowledge of AWS and Cloud infrastructure while improving my communication, teamwork, and time management skills. I also aim to develop hands-on experience in automation, CI/CD, Infrastructure as Code, monitoring, and Cloud security.

### Conclusion

Overall, the internship at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam Company Limited** helped me solidify my professional expertise, improve my working style, and apply AWS concepts to a real-world project. This experience provided a clearer understanding of professional environment requirements and reinforced my career orientation toward becoming a **DevOps Engineer**.
