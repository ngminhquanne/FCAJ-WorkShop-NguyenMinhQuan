In this section, I would like to share some personal feelings, perspectives, and feedback after participating in the First Cloud Journey program at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam Company Limited**. As a **Cloud Infrastructure Engineer** in the project **Smart Healthcare Appointment System**, I had unique experiences and valuable lessons while building an online medical appointment booking system.

## General Evaluation

### 1. Learning and Working Environment
The program environment is friendly, open, and promotes a self-learning spirit. The roadmap is divided into distinct stages, helping me easily track progress and identify focus areas, though the technical requirements of a healthcare project also demanded higher precision regarding system security and availability.

Through labs, workshops, and practical activities, I had the opportunity to get acquainted with various AWS service groups. Directly operating within the Cloud environment made me realize the importance of designing a secure network architecture for patient data.

I propose that the program organize more specialized workshops tailored to specific domains (such as FinTech or HealthTech) to give participants deeper practical perspectives from different industry use cases.

### 2. Mentor and Admin Support
My mentor accompanied and greatly supported me in troubleshooting complex network configurations, executing labs, and deploying the Smart Healthcare Appointment System project. The team admin consistently maintained clear communication, helping me manage my time effectively and complete tasks on schedule.

### 3. Suitability with Professional Background
The internship process perfectly aligned with my career development orientation toward Cloud Infrastructure. Fundamental knowledge of computer networks, databases, and security was directly applied when building the medical appointment management system.

Designing the infrastructure required strict adherence to network partitioning (VPC, Subnet) and data security (Security Group, IAM), allowing me to seamlessly bridge academic theory with operational reality.

### 4. Learning and Skill Development Opportunities
The program provided comprehensive development opportunities in both technical expertise and technical documentation skills, system requirement analysis, and translating concepts into infrastructure architecture for the Smart Healthcare Appointment System.

I proactively combined official AWS documentation, community resources, and AI tools to optimize the research process and resolve emerging challenges during system setup.

### 5. Culture and Teamwork
The program created a cohesive community space. The enthusiastic support from other members when I encountered difficulties with storage or security configurations helped me truly appreciate the value of teamwork in large-scale technology projects.

### 6. Support Policies and Learning Resources
The program's materials and labs served as a solid launchpad. However, I suggest supplementing the learning resources with troubleshooting scenarios so trainees can sharpen their quick-response reflexes when facing real system errors.

## Most Satisfying Aspect

What satisfied me most was the ability to piece together individual services from lab exercises to build a complete system for the Smart Healthcare Appointment System project. This experience provided a thorough perspective on how AWS components interact and support one another to solve real business challenges.

Additionally, community events featuring open sharing from passionate young speakers inspired me to persistently pursue advanced technical paths.

## Suggested Improvements

Based on my practical experience, the program could be further optimized through the following points:

* Add specialized guidelines regarding data security standards for specialized systems (such as healthcare and finance).
* Increase demo sessions or post-mortem/troubleshooting experience-sharing from mentors.
* Provide standardized templates for architecture design diagrams early in the program.

## Program Introduction

I am fully ready to recommend the program to anyone looking to enter the fields of Cloud Computing and AWS. The program possesses high practical value, requiring strong independence, but the achieved results are well worth the effort.
